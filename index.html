Walk With Me (2021)

text-based interactive fiction solo game jam project writing ui & programming CONVERSATIONS BETWEEN A YOUNG GOD AND A HEART OF STONE. amid the war of gods, a young god finds a friend in the mountains. snippets of conversation of a story that has already told / has yet to be finished. Walk With Me is a text-based piece of interactive fiction about storytelling and rememberance.
